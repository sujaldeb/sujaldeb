## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🔵 [Vendor Performance & Inventory Analytics](https://github.com/sujaldeb/vendor-analysis-end-to-end)
> `Analytics` `SQL` `Python` `Power BI` `ML`

Built an end-to-end analytics pipeline on **15M+ liquor distribution records** to evaluate vendor performance, detect overstock risk, and predict underperforming products.

- 📌 **$4M+** in losses from underperforming products identified
- 📌 **80%** of revenue concentrated in top 17 vendors
- 📌 Full pipeline: SQL → Python → ML → Power BI dashboard

---

### 🟣 [E-commerce Business Analytics System — Olist](https://github.com/sujaldeb/E-commerce-Business-Analytics-System-Olist)
> `SQL` `Python` `CTEs` `Window Functions` `Pandas`

Analyzed **1.5M+ transactions** across a multi-vendor marketplace to uncover retention failure, delivery impact on satisfaction, and revenue concentration patterns.

- 📌 **97%** of customers never returned — retention risk surfaced
- 📌 Delivery delays found to be the #1 churn driver
- 📌 Revenue concentration pattern revealed across seller tiers

---

### 🟢 [Used Car Pricing Intelligence System](https://github.com/sujaldeb/Used-Car-Pricing-Intelligence-System)
> `ML` `Scikit-learn` `Python` `EDA` `Feature Engineering`

End-to-end ML system on **370K+ listings** to predict fair vehicle prices, detect overpriced listings, and surface key price variation drivers.

- 📌 **86.8%** R² accuracy on pricing model
- 📌 Overpriced listing detection flagging system built
- 📌 Key drivers: mileage, brand, age, fuel type

---

### 🟡 [Credit Risk & Loan Amount Prediction](https://github.com/sujaldeb/credit-risk-loan-amount-detection)
> `ML` `Classification` `Regression` `LendingClub` `Python`

End-to-end ML pipeline for credit risk classification and loan amount prediction on **2.26M LendingClub records**.

- 📌 **77.4%** ROC-AUC on credit risk detection
- 📌 Dual-model: classification (default risk) + regression (loan amount)
- 📌 Handles severe class imbalance with SMOTE + threshold tuning

---

### 🟠 [Property Price Prediction](https://github.com/sujaldeb/Property-Price-Prediction)
> `ML` `Ridge Regression` `Scikit-learn` `Jupyter`

Machine learning model predicting property prices using Ridge Regression with **90% accuracy**.

- 📌 **90%** model accuracy achieved
- 📌 Feature engineering on location, size, amenities
- 📌 Regularization approach to prevent overfitting

---

### 🔴 [E-Commerce Customer Intelligence Platform](https://github.com/sujaldeb/E-Commerce-Customer-Intelligence-Platform)
> `Analytics` `RFM` `Forecasting` `Python` `ML`

End-to-end retail analytics and forecasting platform on **1.06M transactions** — customer segmentation, retention analysis, revenue forecasting, and product association insights.

- 📌 RFM-based customer segmentation with actionable clusters
- 📌 Revenue forecasting with time-series modeling
- 📌 Product association rules for cross-sell strategy
